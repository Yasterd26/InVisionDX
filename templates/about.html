Traditional Diagnostic Limitations

Medical professionals face significant challenges in diagnostic processes:

  • Paper-based records cause delays in accessing patient histories
  • Limited accessibility to specialist expertise, especially in remote areas

Our Technology

InVisionDX integrates cutting-edge AI and machine learning technologies to transform medical diagnostics:

CNN Architecture

Our custom Convolutional Neural Networks are trained on millions of medical images to identify patterns invisible to the human eye. These specialized networks achieve:

  • 98.7% accuracy in COVID-19 detection from CT scans
  • Multi-layered feature extraction for nuanced diagnosis
  • Rapid processing (under 3 seconds per image)

Transfer Learning

We employ advanced transfer learning to adapt pre-trained models for specific diagnostic tasks:

  • Foundation models fine-tuned with specialized medical datasets
  • Capability to work with limited training examples
  • Continuous improvement through federated learning

Explainable AI

Our systems provide transparent explanations for diagnostic suggestions:

  • Heat-map visualization of key diagnostic areas
  • Confidence scores with probability distributions
  • Comparable case references from anonymized database
